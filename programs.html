Program FAQs

Are programs free?

Yes! All YouthSpark programs and support services are 100% free for youth and families.

Can I attend remotely?

Most workshops and mentorship sessions are available online via Google Meet/Zoom across India.

Is there a minimum age or requirement?

Youth ages 12–25 (or caregivers/teachers) are eligible. Some programs have specific focus age groups—ask us which fits you best.
