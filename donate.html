Donation FAQ

Is my donation tax-exempt?

Yes, YouthSpark is a registered NGO and all donations above ₹500 are eligible for 80G tax exemption. A receipt/certificate is issued for every donation.

Can I donate from outside India?

Currently, we accept only Indian contributions via UPI or bank transfer.

What impact does my donation create?

Your money directly supports mentorship, recovery camps, education kits, and mental health helpline for youth in need.

How do I get a receipt?

Forward your payment confirmation/name to donate@youthspark.org and you’ll receive your official receipt within 2 business days.
